# Product Brief

Private Men's Health Coach is a calm, privacy-first mobile companion for daily recovery, confidence, learning, and self-tracking.

The app helps answer five questions every day:

- How am I today?
- What matters most today?
- What should I do today?
- Is there any risk or caution?
- Is there something new worth learning?

The MVP is not a medical product. It supports education, pattern tracking, conservative routine guidance, and preparation for better decisions.

## Product Principles

- Privacy-first by default.
- Useful without wearables or integrations.
- Daily use should take under two minutes.
- Manual logging must be easier than opening notes.
- Recommendations must explain why they appear.
- Safety and low-data states must be conservative.
- No body shaming, performance shaming, diagnosis, or medical certainty.
